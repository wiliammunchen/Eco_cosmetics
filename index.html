About our brand

About our brand

Our cosmetics are made from natural ingredients and packaged with respect for the planet. Shampoo bars, soaps and labels rely on recycled paper, while creams and oils come in reusable glass containers.

We reject synthetics and microplastics, and we design every pack so it can be returned, recycled or repurposed. Caring for your body should not harm the place we all share.

    About the creators

    Victoria creates the formulas for every candle, powder and oil. Her work grew from years of studying herbs, natural care and the quiet rituals of mountain life.

    Together with her husband, she also volunteers to rescue wild birds and supports rehabilitation initiatives in the Carpathian region. The brand exists to fund that care as much as it exists to make beautiful products.
